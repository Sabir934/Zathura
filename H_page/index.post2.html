POCO C75 5G Launched Under ₹10K the Best Budget 5G Phone of 2025.


Article posted on / 12/04/2025

India's budget smartphone arena just received a big shakeup with the unveiling of the POCO C75 5G. Starting below ₹10,000, the phone gives consumers what so many budget-seekers have been craving: consistent 5G connectivity, minimalistic aesthetics, and normal day-to-day performance without eating a hole through your wallet.


💢 Initial Impressions: Silver Stardust That Sparkles.


At first glance, the Silver Stardust edition is making a strong fashion statement. The back panel features a reflective finish that catches light at the perfect angle, providing a high-end look that's unusual in this category. POCO has done a good job of balancing form and function.

🔗 Key Specs at a Glance:


  • Display : A big HD+ screen ideal for binge-watching, browsing, or gaming.

  • Processor : Equipped with a powerful chipset that can easily manage day-to-day tasks.

  • RAM & Storage : 4 GB RAM and 64 GB storage (expandable) – perfect for casual users and multitasking.

  • Connectivity: 5G support makes you future-proof without burning a hole in your pocket.

  • Battery : A battery that lasts the whole day comfortably.


💡 Performance That Keeps Up


Though it is no gaming monster, the POCO C75 5G performs the common apps like WhatsApp, YouTube, Instagram, and light multitasking without any jerk. The 4 GB of RAM works okay to maintain everything smooth, and POCO's UI optimization truly pays dividends here.


🧿 Camera Setup: For Everyday Moments


The camera setup is quite decent for this segment, but good enough to take clean daylight photos and satisfactory selfies. It is not designed to rival flagship cameras, but for social media postings and casual clicks, it is ample.


🔥 Battery Life That Lasts


One of its most robust areas is battery life. Even with 5G activated, the phone comfortably lasts a full day under moderate usage, due to its processor and optimally implemented software.

4. Xiaomi HyperOS


Uncover a brighter, faster, and smarter smartphone experience with Xiaomi HyperOS, which is fine-tuned for effortless performance and personalization features.


5. 5160 mAh Battery + 10 W Fast Charging


Use continuously with a huge 5160mAh battery, complemented by 10W fast charging for rapid replenishments to get you through the day.


6. Marble Flow Design


Create a dramatic style statement with the sophisticated Marble Flow design, uniting distinctive textures with a luxurious finish.
