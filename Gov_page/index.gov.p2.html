SSC JE Application Process 2025


Step 1: Go to the official SSC website (https://ssc.gov.in/).

Step 2: Open the registration link for SSC JE in a new window.

Step 3: Click on the "New User/Register Now" link.

Step 4: Provide basic details like name, parent name, date of birth, email ID, and mobile number.

Step 5: Submit the online registration form. Make sure to verify all details before submission. You'll receive a Registration ID.

Step 6: Upload your photograph and signature according to SSC guidelines.

Photograph: Taken against a white or light-colored background, size between 4 kb and 12 kb, resolution of 100*120 pixels.

Signature: Black or blue ink on a white sheet, scanned copy in jpg format, size between 1 kb and 12 kb, resolution of 40*60 pixels.

Step 7: Log in with your registered ID and password to complete Part II of the application form.

Step 8: Preview the entire application for any errors before final submission. Once submitted, the form cannot be edited.

Step 9: Click on "Final Submit" and pay the required application fee online via Net-Banking, Credit/Debit cards, BHIM, UPI, etc.

Step 10: It's recommended to download and print copies of the submitted application form for SSC JE 2025.

SSC JE Selection Process


The SSC JE selection process consists of three principal stages: Paper 1, Paper 2, and document verification. Candidates must know the process thoroughly to prepare for every stage and get selected. Let's discuss the selection stages of SSC JE below:


  • SSC JE 2025 Paper 1

    • Paper 1 comprises 200 questions with multiple choices in general awareness, reasoning and logical ability, and specific subjects in the opted discipline. For every correct response, 1 mark is awarded, and for every incorrect response, 0.25 marks are deducted. The duration of the exam is 2 hours. Paper 1 aims to examine the basic knowledge of the candidate in general awareness and in their technical field.

  • SSC JE 2025 Paper 2

    • Paper 2 is devoted to the selected subject by the candidate and comprises 100 questions. They try to find the candidate's in-depth understanding of central subjects. Every correct response is awarded 3 marks, and incorrect responses incur a 1-mark penalty. This is a 2-hour examination as well.

  • SSC JE 2025 Document Verification

    • The candidates who qualify for Paper 1 and Paper 2 will proceed to the document verification phase. Here, they need to present all original certificates and documents for verification.

  • Medical Examination

    • Candidates also need to undergo a medical test to ensure that they meet the health requirements of the respective departments.

SSC JE 2025 Exam Pattern


SSC JE 2025 Exam will be of two papers. Paper 1 will be for 200 marks and paper 2 will be of 300 marks. Both paper 1 and paper 2 are MCQ. For each question attempted wrong by a candidate in paper 1, 0.25 marks will be deducted and in paper 2 there will be a penalty of 1 mark.

SSC JE 2025 Preparation Tips


SSC JE 2025 Preparation Tips

To excel in the SSC JE 2025 exam, one requires a good preparation strategy. Success in the exam demands constant practice, a well-directed approach, and appropriate guidance. Following are some useful tips for effective preparation:


  • Understand the Syllabus – Begin by going through the entire syllabus so that you can comprehend the difficulty of the exam and what you must study. You can form your study plan from the syllabus so that you remain on track.


  • Make a Study Schedule – Prepare a study schedule that provides sufficient time to every subject. Good time management will enhance your preparation and assist you in scoring higher.

  • Use the Correct Study Material – Select study materials that are suggested by professionals and encompass the whole syllabus. Online lectures, reference books, handwritten notes, and crash courses are used to study effectively.

  • Practice Regularly – Regular practice is the most important way to enhance your performance. Practice problems, rehearse major concepts, and work on formulas to build your skills.

  • Take Mock Tests – Mock tests are an excellent means of measuring your strengths and weaknesses. Through taking these tests, you can gauge your improvement and modify your study plan to perform better.

  • Remain Confident – During your preparation, remain confident in your hard work. Confidence will minimize exam anxiety and prepare you to deal with tough questions.
