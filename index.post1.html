Motorola G35 5G Launch New Mobile Phone Experience Speed with 128 GB Storage & 4 GB RAM.

article posted on / 12/04/2025


In a universe where the new flagship phone is worth an arm and a leg, Motorola still defends budget buyers with sturdy, classy smartphones — and the Motorola G35 5G (Leaf Green, 128 GB | 4 GB RAM) is the perfect exemplar.


💡 First Impressions: A Fresh Look

Straight out of the packaging, the Leaf Green version of the G35 5G is a head-turner. It's a subtle, matte finish with a clean look — no flash wasted, just clever style. It's light and solid, and it feels wonderful in hand, and it's slim enough for one-handed operation.


⚡ Performance: 5G on a Budget

Driven by a MediaTek Dimensity chip, accompanied by 4 GB RAM, this phone is not meant to fight it out with top-end flagships — but that's not the idea. For everyday use such as messaging, video streaming, browsing, and even casual gaming, it's responsive and silky smooth. And yes, 5G support makes you future-proof in terms of connectivity, offering you quicker downloads and smoother streaming even while on the move.


🧿 Display & Multimedia

The G35 has a 6.5-inch HD+ screen with good brightness and color for media viewing. It won't wow you like an AMOLED display, but it's doing well in this segment. YouTube watching, Insta scrolling, or reading — all are comfortable on the eyes. The audio is crisp and loud enough for recreational listening, and with a headphone jack still available (yes, indeed!) it's a plus for audiophiles who prefer wired sound.


📸 Camera: Clicks that Count

The phone has a triple rear camera module, a primary 50MP sensor that clicks quite decent pictures in daylight. Night mode aids in low-light conditions, but miracles are not expected. The 8MP front-facing selfie camera performs satisfactorily for casual shots and video calls. At this price point, the camera configuration is more than decent — particularly for social media sharing.


🔋 It Just Keeps Going

With a 5000mAh battery, the G35 5G is an all-day phone. Even with intense use — calls, browsing, social media, and videos — it won't have you scrambling for a charger at bedtime. USB-C fast charging is also supported, so filling up is fast and easy.


💾 Storage & Software

You have 128 GB of built-in storage, which is more than enough for apps, photos, and video — and there's expandable storage through microSD too. Motorola's close-to-stock Android experience is so clean and free of bloat that many users love it. No pesky ads, no redundant apps — just nice, clean simplicity.

Take vibrant photos with the 50 MP Quad Pixel camera, capturing every shot with stunning clarity. Expand your view with the 8 MP ultra-wide lens and fit 4x more into the frame. Whether it's a flawless selfie with the 16 MP front camera or a perfectly focused moment thanks to laser autofocus, every photo is a masterpiece. Plus, it captures life in its purest form with the segment's only 4K video recording for crystal-clear videos.


4. Powerful UNISOC T760 5G Processor.

Experience next-level 5G performance with the Moto g35 5G, driven by the segment's most powerful UNISOC T760 octa-core processor. From smooth 4K Video recording, clearer video calls to advanced AI photo editing with Google Photos, it ensures fast speeds and effortless multitasking. Power through your day with the performance you need, right in the palm of your hand.


5. In-Built 4 GB RAM + 128 GB Storage.

Enjoy effortless multitasking with 4GB of built-in RAM, allowing you to switch between apps seamlessly as they stay ready in the background. Need a performance boost? Always active by default, it ensures that multitasking remains fluid, adding extra RAM when you need it most.
